About This Project

M3M MANSIONS Is A Luxury Residential Development That Has Newly Opened In The Fast-growing City Of Gurgaon. In The Beautifully Created Flats, They Have Been Given An Elevated Living Experience In The Lap Of Nature Developed By M3m India. Elevated Living Is Known In Gurugram Promising Location, Where You Will Welcome Elegance. Presenting The Finest Residential Complex That Promises A Taste Of City Life. M3M MANSIONS Sector 113 Is A Big Tract Of Open Land That Is Part Of Residential Development To Supply You And Your Family With Enough Open Space For Plenty Of Daylight And Fresh Air. It's A Wonderful Blend Of Strategic Location And Improved Features, With Fresh Air Around You.

M3M Mansion is an upcoming ultra-luxury residential project by M3M India, strategically located in Sector 113 along the Dwarka Expressway in Gurgaon. This development offers a range of 3.5 and 4.5 BHK high-end residences, with sizes varying from approximately 2,100 to 7,000 square feet. Residents can enjoy a 9+9 hole chip and putt golfing landscape spread over 1.25 acres, 7.5 acres of central vistas with fluid walking pathways, rejuvenating aquatic features, shaded benches, gazebos, and towering trees to enhance air quality.

Project Highlights:-

Imported marble flooring in living spaces and grand lobbies

Thoughtfully designed spacious layouts with premium finishes

Exclusive 18-hole golf course within the community

Massive 100,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with luxury amenities

High-rise towers offering panoramic views of Delhi green

Resort-style living with a pool, gym, and sports facilities

Strategically located on Dwarka Expressway for seamless connectivity
