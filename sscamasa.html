SS Camasa

About This Project

SS Camasa, strategically located in Sector 90, Gurugram, is a premium residential project that epitomizes modern living. The development offers an array of meticulously designed apartments, ranging from spacious luxurious 4 BHK homes, tailored to meet the needs of contemporary lifestyles. Spread across a thoughtfully planned land parcel, the project combines elegant architecture with functional layouts to deliver homes that maximize space and natural light. With a focus on convenience and comfort, residents can enjoy state-of-the-art amenities, including a swimming pool, fitness center, landscaped gardens, and dedicated play areas for children. The location of SS Camasa is one of its standout features, providing seamless connectivity to major hubs such as NH-8, Dwarka Expressway, and key IT and business districts. Proximity to reputed schools, healthcare facilities, and entertainment zones ensures a lifestyle of unparalleled ease and sophistication, making it an ideal choice for families and professionals alike.

One of the residential developments in Sector 90 Gurgaon, SS Camasa (also known as the Luxury Project Gurgaon) by the SS Group, is home to some of the top residential developments in the nation. This project, which is situated in Sector 90, is ideal for working families since it promises its residents good access to the business district, retail centers, schools, hospitals, and other important infrastructure. The best lifestyle amenities can be found at SS Camasa Sector 90 Gurgaon, including a clubhouse, swimming pool, exquisite landscaping, a quality gym, and a children’s playground. Many of these amenities are international. The project’s location, services, and facilities are distinctive, drawing in high-end, budget-conscious customers from Gurgaon’s micro market.
