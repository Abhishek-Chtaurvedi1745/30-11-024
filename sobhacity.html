Sobha City

About This Project

Live Welcome to Sobha City the premium residential project developed by Sobha Limited in Sector 109 of Gurgaon. With 6 towers over 1,548 apartments ranging from 2, 3 & 4 bedrooms, Sobha City offers a spacious and luxurious living space equipped with modern amenities that cater to your every need. A total of 8.9 acres are dedicated to open and green space, with over 4000 trees and one acre of waterbodies that contribute to the preservation of a temperate climate.

At Sobha City, we understand the importance of modern amenities and features in today's lifestyle. That's why we have equipped each apartment with a clubhouse, swimming pool, jogging track, gymnasium, and a multipurpose hall that caters to your every need. The apartments are designed to be spacious and well-ventilated, allowing natural light to flood in. The new towers are spaciously built to give exclusive views of the sprawling urban park.
