About This Project

Luxurious living is available at BPTP's planned high-rise residential complex in Sector 102, Gurgaon. Spacious 2 and 3 BHK residences with contemporary fixtures and excellent craftsmanship are part of the project. High-end features including round-the-clock security, power backup, a swimming pool, a gym, a running track, and indoor and outdoor sports facilities are the main focus of the development. Because of its ideal location along the Dwarka Expressway, this residential development offers great access, particularly to Indira Gandhi International Airport and the future Diplomatic Enclave. With schools, hospitals, and shopping malls close by, the region is expanding quickly and is perfect for both professionals and families. The building also encourages natural sunlight and ventilation, providing a modern and comfortable lifestyle. BPTP Lutyens 102 offers a range of premium amenities, including a world-class clubhouse, a state-of-the-art fitness center, a spa, and an infinity-edge swimming pool. Residents can enjoy multiple sports facilities such as tennis, basketball, and badminton courts, as well as jogging tracks and landscaped gardens that promote an active lifestyle. A multi-tier security system ensures the highest level of safety, providing residents with peace of mind. BPTP Lutyens 102 is designed to be a perfect blend of elegance, luxury, and convenience. With its high-end specifications, world-class amenities, and prime location, this development redefines upscale living in Gurgaon, offering residents an unparalleled lifestyle experience.
