About This Project

Experience the Opulence of Trump Tower 2 Gurgaon Sector 69 Imagine a world of unparalleled luxury, where every detail is crafted to elevate your lifestyle. Welcome to Trump Tower 2, Gurgaon Sector 69, where the boundaries of sophistication are redefined. Indulge in the Finest Amenities and Interiors

As the tallest twin towers in North India, Trump Tower 2 Gurgaon soars to an impressive height of 200 meters. This landmark development spans over 60 acres of prime land and consists of 254 exclusive units, setting new standards for super-luxury living in the region. The grandeur and sophistication of these residences make Trump Tower 2 Gurgaon a coveted address, dominating the skyline of the Millennium City. Strategically located, Trump Tower 2 Gurgaon is surrounded by leading multinational corporations, upscale hotels, vibrant local markets, and key transportation hubs, offering residents unmatched convenience and connectivity. The interiors of Trump Tower 2 Gurgaon are thoughtfully designed by the renowned Italian designer Matteo Nunziati, ensuring each residence exudes elegance and modern sophistication. With Shapoorji Pallonji Group as the construction partner, the project benefits from the expertise of one of India’s most trusted builders, ensuring exceptional quality and precision.

Specification of the project below:-

Trump Tower 2 in Sector 69, Gurgaon, is a high-end residential development by Tribeca Creators LLP. Situated in Sector 69 SPR, Vatika Chowk, Gurgaon, the project features a fully glass façade and offers Trump White Glove Services, ensuring an exceptional standard of luxury living. Trump Towers Delhi NCR offers 3 and 4 BHK Double Height Residences, ranging from 3000 sq.ft. to 5000 sq.ft., dominating the skyline of the millennium city. The development comprises two towers, spanning 3.5 acres with a total of 270 units spread across G+50 floors. The location is well-connected, surrounded by multinational corporations, hotels, local markets, and transport hubs. The Trump Towers 2 payment plan is flexible, following a 30:40:30 structure. The interiors are designed by renowned Italian designer Matteo Nunziati, and the construction partner is Shapoorji Pallonji, known for their expertise in global luxury properties.
