About This Project

Puri Diplomatic Residences, located in Sector 111 along the Dwarka Expressway, is a prestigious and luxurious residential project designed to offer a refined living experience. It is spread across a sprawling 9-acre campus and consists of six towers, including five luxury towers and one Ikonic Luxury Tower. The design of the development is centered around providing comfort and elegance, with ample space, high-end finishes, and contemporary architecture. The apartments range from 3 and 4 BHK configurations, with sizes varying between 2,282 and 3,380 square feet, ensuring residents have plenty of space for modern living.

Situated in a highly strategic location, Puri Constructions is coming with a new launch of Puri Diplomatic Residences. Spanning over 9 acres, Puri Diplomatic Residences offers 3 and 4 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 2,282 to 3,380 square feet. The development is equipped with a range of luxury amenities, including a clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, landscaped gardens, and more, creating a comfortable and upscale living environment. Strategically located, it offers excellent connectivity to Delhi, Gurgaon, Manesar, NH48, and IGI Airport Puri Diplomatic Residences is an ultra-luxury residential project situated in Sector 111, Gurgaon, along the Dwarka Expressway. This development comprises six high-rise towers, including five luxury towers with four flats per core and one exclusive Ikonic Luxury Tower featuring two flats per core. The apartments are designed with expansive balconies and large deck areas, offering residents ample space to relax and enjoy the outdoors. Interiors are equipped with air conditioning and imported marble flooring, enhancing the overall ambiance and comfort. The project also boasts a double-height air-conditioned lobby and landscaped stilts with a grand drop-off zone, adding to its grandeur.
