About This Project

Engulfed with royal creations and a serene ambience, Golf Course Road, a plush avenue of Gurgaon aims to offer an ideal living culture to millennials. Soon going to be inaugurating another one of its resplendent creations, Godrej Properties is all set to craft a benchmark amongst an extensive range of real estate clientele at a global level. Godrej Miraya is a brand-new household project where residents attain a great opportunity to own a super-luxurious home. Godrej Miraya Sector 43 Gurgaon is an ideal kind of development for all those who wish to be enclosed with aristocracy and glory. People will become the permanent dwellers of a neighbourhood which is associated with vibrancy, present in the form of elite landmarks and great lifestyle destinations. Clubhouse at Tulip Monsella is located on the lobby level. The Clubhouse offers the Mini Theatre, Cards Lounge, Snooker Lounge, Banquet Hall, Cigar Lounge, Swimming Pool, Kids Pool, Gaming Lounge, Gym.

Gurgaon, searching for the best living standard? Well, look no further than Godrej Miraya Sector 43 Gurgaon. This luxurious project by Godrej is the new-age landmark of the apartment complex. When people say every little thing in a house shouts out class and comfort, that’s exactly what Godrej Miraya symbolizes. Perfectly situated in Godrej Sector 43 Gurgaon, this residential property provides ultra Luxury Project Gurgaon with the best of luxury and accessibility. Modern structure and exclusive design make the property a favourite among connoisseurs of a comfort-oriented way of life. It has all the charm of a quiet landscaped environment for those who are in search of a peaceful environment and all the energy of a city for those who are in search of an urban environment, and all this is accompanied by magnificent facilities and carefully designed and built homes.
